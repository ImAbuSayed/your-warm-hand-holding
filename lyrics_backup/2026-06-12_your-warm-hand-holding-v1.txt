[Hook]
Shadows dance across the hardwood floor
I pull you closer than I did before
Just a silent look that leaves me wanting more
And your warm hand holding onto mine

[Verse 1]
The midnight moon is spilling through the blinds
Tracing every curve my fingers want to find
We left the noise of the rushing world behind
Losing track of hours as our souls intertwine

[Pre-Chorus]

This is exactly where the deepest parts begin
No need to rush it, let the quiet pull us in
I feel the fire underneath your subtle grin

[Chorus]
